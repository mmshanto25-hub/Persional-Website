About Me

I’m Shanto — a graphics designer and photographer based in Savar, Dhaka. I’ve delivered work on Fiverr, Upwork, and direct clients through my studio Idea’s Grove. My toolkit includes Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Lightroom, with experience in brand strategy and marketing collaterals.

As a photo editor, I’m meticulous with color science and skin tones. As a designer, I focus on clarity, hierarchy, and a strong point of view that helps clients stand out.
